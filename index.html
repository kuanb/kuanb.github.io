About

I'm a recent gradaute of MIT's Master of City Planning Transportation Systems Planning program; now at Hacker School in New York City. My interests lie in implementing digital technology in engaging spatial (usually transport-related) issues. That's a roundabout way of saying that seeing things end up on a map helps make me feel like the work I do has some more tangible and clear benefit to society. I have extensive international experience, from Mexico City to Dhaka, Bangladesh. That said, midwest American holds a special place in my heart, and I hope to eventually use my skills and experiences in addressing issues unique to it. At present, I am also continuing the development and implementation of my ongoing/never-ending project Flocktracker, with all my awesome teammates at Urban Launchpad.
